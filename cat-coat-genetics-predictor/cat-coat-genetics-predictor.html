Genetics legend & assumptions

Twelve independent loci, assorting independently:

  • Orange (X-linked): O orange, o non-orange. Females OO/Oo/oo → orange/tortie/eumelanin. Males O/o → orange/eumelanin. XXY tortie males excluded.
  • B locus: B black > b chocolate > c cinnamon. Only affects eumelanin.
  • Dilute: dd dilutes pigment (black→blue, chocolate→lilac, cinnamon→fawn, red→cream).
  • Agouti: A_ shows tabby pattern on eumelanin; aa solid. Orange always shows pattern (agouti cannot suppress phaeomelanin).
  • Tabby T: M mackerel > m classic.
  • Ticked: T_ overrides body striping with ticking; epistatic to T-locus and Sp.
  • Spotted modifier: P_ turns mackerel into spotted (only visible if T_, ti/ti, Sp).
  • Silver: I_ inhibits pigment in hair shaft. Tabby + silver = silver tabby; solid + silver = smoke.
  • Colorpoint: ff (recessive) = Siamese-like colorpoint, blue eyes.
  • White spotting: dosage matters — 0/1/2 copies of S → none / some / high white. Tortie + white = calico.
  • Dominant white: W_ masks everything → all-white cat (often blue eyes & deafness, not modeled).
  • Long hair: ll recessive longhair.
